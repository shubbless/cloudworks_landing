Why is CloudWorks important?


Diagnostic tests are widely used, imperative and valuable at the frontlines, but a huge opportunity is being lost at the moment. We lack real-time digitized diagnoses. This loss presents a systemic health system failure that won’t get solved by existing players in the diagnostic ecosystem because everyone’s prerogatives are misaligned. Dimagi and FIND want to move the ecosystem of digital diagnostics forward by investing across all phases of at-scale digital diagnostics in the short term and the long term for enabling innovation.

Open Source

CloudWorks is an open source global good middleware to ensure all stakeholders in the diagnostic ecosystem can interoperate with digitized readings of RDTs in near real-time.

Increased Confidence

CloudWorks’ remote validation capabilities ensure readings of rapid diagnostic tests are accurate, increasing confidence in your team’s results and maximizing their impact. It will also provide an architecture and design that supports new RDTs (ie. COVID-19) to be added easily in the future.

Disease Surveillance

The backend contains components to address the infrastructure and incentive barriers to using & scaling eDiagnostics and eReaders. The functionality enables the creation of datasets and a metering system for eReaders and eDiagnostics to go-to-market.

RDTs & eDiagnostics

CloudWorks supports two types of diagnostics: Rapid Diagnostic Tests and electronic diagnostics.

eReaders for RDTs

Near Term

Rapid Diagnostic Tests are widely used and highly impactful today, but without the ability to scan, validate, and digitize their readings at the frontlines in an interoperable and globally supported toolkit, we end up leaving significant impact unrealized. Stuck with once-off solutions for individual programs that do not, and can not, take advantage of prior learnings nor turnkey integrations. This has led programs, manufacturers, data collection companies, eReaders, and funders to operate in silos rather than collaboratively to achieve the same end goal to grow impact.

Learn More
eDiagnostics

Future

Over time, we will begin to see more preventable diseases able to be diagnosed solely using an app on a smartphone. In order for these futuristic methods of diagnosis to scale in the market and support frontline programs in the near future, various stakeholders need to have come together to resolve the health system leakages and ensure appropriate APIs, softwares, and governance is available to build upon. The work to scale eReaders will also help shape the market for eDiagnostics.
